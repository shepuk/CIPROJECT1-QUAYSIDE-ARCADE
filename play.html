Welcome

Welcome to Quayside Arcade, home to thousands of games and activities, plus amazing food and drinks. Situated on Newcastle's picturesque city centre riverside, in an open-plan building and full to the brim with all kinds of technology. On this page you'll find out about all of our equipment and facilities.
We're a diverse bunch and proud of it. We're all about inclusivity - everyone should be able to have fun and meet new people in a safe space. So come and meet the team, get involved and wheather you're competing, spectating or just enjoying the Quayside view with a coffee, we'll be thrilled you see you!

Play

NEW - Playstation 5 & Xbox Series X now available!

The freshest titles on the newest hardware. We've got a substantial (and growing!) collection of games from the last two decades. Huge 4K displays allow you and your spectators to really immerse yourselves into the game. If you prefer sitting at a desk to play with a keyboard & mouse, don't worry - we've for your back with a dedicated LAN area, full of high-end PC gaming systems. Our modern-gaming selection includes:

  • NEW PS5 & Xbox Series X
  • PS4, PS3 & PS2
  • Xbox One, Xbox 360 & Xbox OG
  • Switch & Wii
  • High-end PCs

And there's more. No time limits or booking necessary for large portions of our equipment. So what are oyu waiting for? We can't wait to see you.
